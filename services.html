Our Services

Beyond products: we offer expertise, customization, and partnerships for businesses and individuals.

What We Offer

At VerdeGold, we believe in providing complete culinary solutions. From individual gourmet enthusiasts to large restaurant chains, we have services tailored to meet your needs.

Restaurant & Hospitality Supply

We provide bulk purchasing options for restaurants, hotels, and catering services. Our dedicated account managers work with you to create custom product mixes, ensure consistent supply, and offer competitive pricing for volume orders.

Custom Gift Boxes & Corporate Gifting

Create memorable gifts with our custom curation service. Choose from our products to create personalized gift boxes for employees, clients, or special events. We handle packaging, branding, and delivery nationwide.

Culinary Consultation

Our team of food experts can help you develop recipes, create menus, or troubleshoot culinary challenges. We offer virtual and in-person consultations for home cooks and professional chefs alike.

Subscription Services

Never run out of your favorites with our flexible subscription plans. Choose your products, set your delivery frequency, and enjoy discounted prices plus free shipping. Pause or cancel anytime.

Sustainability Consulting

Leverage our expertise in sustainable sourcing and operations. We help businesses implement eco-friendly practices, reduce food waste, and communicate their sustainability efforts to customers.
