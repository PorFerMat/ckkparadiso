VerdeGold Launches Carbon-Neutral Shipping Initiative

VerdeGold today announced its commitment to carbon-neutral shipping across all orders. The company has partnered with Climate Neutral to measure, offset, and reduce its carbon footprint, making it one of the first gourmet food brands to achieve this milestone.

New Tropical Dried Fruit Line Wins "Best New Product" Award

VerdeGold's new line of organic tropical dried fruits has been recognized with the "Best New Product" award at the 2023 Natural Products Expo. The judges praised the product's authentic flavor, sustainable packaging, and innovative dehydration process that preserves nutrients.

VerdeGold Expands Partnership with Southeast Asian Rice Farmers

The company has announced an expanded partnership with a cooperative of 200 smallholder rice farmers in Thailand. The new 5-year agreement guarantees fair prices, provides agricultural training, and supports community development projects in the region.

Founder Maya Chen Named to "30 Under 30" Food & Drink List

VerdeGold co-founder Maya Chen has been recognized in Forbes' "30 Under 30" list for Food & Drink. The award acknowledges her leadership in building a sustainable gourmet food brand that has seen 300% growth over the past two years.
