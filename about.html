About VerdeGold

Our journey from farm to table, bringing you the finest gourmet products since 2015.

Experienced Leadership

Since 2002, CKK Paradiso has been providing high-quality products to supermarket chains, large distributors, and commissaries in corrections facilities. Over the years, we have accumulated valuable experiences that enable us to effectively adapt and handle all aspects of the supply chain while navigating the changing economic and market conditions. We are responsible for manufacturing, sourcing, importing, logistics, and distribution. We own or partner with overseas factories to efficiently bring our products to consumers across California, Texas, and the East Coast. CKK Paradiso is committed to providing best service for all phases of business.

Indulge in our carefully crafted food products. We offer a diverse product line of sauces, coconut drinks, premium rice, and dried fruits. They range from conventional to innovative products. We source quality ingredients, and each of our fine products speaks for itself in terms of flavor, quality, and value.
