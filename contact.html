Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We currently ship to over 30 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You'll see accurate shipping costs at checkout based on your address.

Are your products really organic?

Absolutely. All VerdeGold products are certified organic by the USDA and equivalent international organizations. We work exclusively with certified organic farmers and producers.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team. After that window, your order enters our fulfillment system and cannot be changed.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, retailers, and other businesses. Please select "Wholesale/Partnership" in the contact form above or email wholesale@verdegold.com for more information.
